MY FOOD PYRAMID

Welcome to your food pyramid!
Green shelf: Vegetables, salad and fruit - Eat plenty of these, up to 6-7 servings a day.
Brown shelf: Starchy foods - Wholemeal cereals and breads, potatoes, pasta, rice. Wholegrain is best.
Blue shelf: Dairy - Milk, yoghurt and cheese. Choose reduced-fat or low-fat.
Yellow shelf - Meat, poultry, fish, eggs, beans and nuts. Choose lean options and have fish at least twice a week – one being oily.
Orange shelf: Fats, spreads and oils - Have small amounts and choose unsaturated or reduced-fat options.
Red shelf: We call these 'treats' - foods that have high amounts of salt, fat and sugar. This is the least important shelf. Have a treat once or twice a week.
